NEW — ABAP ON RELATIONSHIPS, WHERE THERE IS ANY
The code icon lives on joins because only a join carries the ON fields. A
relationship is a business statement and often shares no key at all. Where a
relationship does happen to be joinable, </> now appears on it too; where it
does not, nothing appears, rather than offering a SELECT that could never run.
It is suppressed on cluster tables entirely — PCL1 and PCL2 share a key and
SQL would join them happily, which would still tell you nothing.
