Privacy Policy — ERP Galaxy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

ERP Galaxy collects no data whatsoever. The app has no account, no analytics, no advertising, and no network connection. It cannot send your information anywhere, because it never sends anything anywhere.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no sign-up, no login, no tracking, no analytics SDK, no advertising identifier, and no crash-reporting service.

Network access

ERP Galaxy makes no network requests of any kind. Every one of its 2,033 reference records ships inside the app itself. The app works fully in Airplane Mode. You can verify this: turn on Airplane Mode and the app behaves identically.

Data stored on your device

The app remembers a few preferences locally so it behaves the way you left it:

  • Your favourite tables
  • Your locked board
  • Your reading mode (Classic or Fun)

These are stored on your device only, using standard iOS local storage. They are never transmitted, and they are deleted when you delete the app.

Spotlight

The app adds its own reference content to your device's Spotlight index so you can search for a table from iOS Search. This index is created and held on your device by iOS. No personal information is indexed, and nothing leaves the device.

Children

ERP Galaxy is a professional reference tool. It collects nothing from anyone, of any age.

Changes

If a future version ever collects anything, this policy will be updated before that version ships, and the change will be described in the App Store release notes.
