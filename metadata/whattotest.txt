NEW — CLUSTER TABLES ARE NOW THEIR OWN CLASS
PCL3 and PCL4 were missing from the app entirely and PCL1 had no description
at all. All four are now there, along with HRPY_RGDIR and P2RX_RT.

More importantly, the app used to list key fields and derived joins on these
tables as though you could SELECT them. You cannot. Open PCL2 and you should
now see, before anything else, a block saying no SELECT will read this data.
